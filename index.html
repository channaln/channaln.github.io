About

Building infrastructure
that doesn't fail.

I'm a Systems Engineer at KUWAITNET in Kuwait with 4+ years building and automating enterprise infrastructure. My focus is Red Hat OpenShift clusters, Ansible Automation Platform deployments, and middleware environments — the kind of work that happens at telecom scale where five-nines availability isn't aspirational, it's contractual. Before KUWAITNET, I built my foundation at VS Information Systems managing VMware clusters, Pacemaker HA setups, and Azure infrastructure. I hold the RHCSA, RHCE, and Red Hat Certified OpenShift Administrator certifications and a degree in Network Security from South Eastern University of Sri Lanka.
